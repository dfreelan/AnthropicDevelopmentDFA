BUG REPORT
==========

Project: paginator.py, app.py
Working directory: PLACEHOLDER

Summary:
  When the total number of items is not evenly divisible by per_page,
  the last page of results is silently dropped. Users cannot access
  the final items.

Steps to reproduce:
  1. Run: python3 app.py 1 10
     - Shows Page 1 of 2 but there are 25 products
     - 25 items / 10 per page should be 3 pages, not 2
  2. Run: python3 app.py 3 10
     - Page 3 shows zero items even though products 21-25 exist

Expected behavior:
  total_pages should be 3 for 25 items at 10 per page.
  Page 3 should show the remaining 5 items.

Actual behavior:
  total_pages reports 2. The last 5 products are inaccessible
  through the pagination API.
